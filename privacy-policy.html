5 Chrome Extension Permissions

Our extension requires specific permissions to function. Here's exactly why we need each one:

activeTab

Access the OnlyFans page you're currently viewing when you click the extension icon. This allows us to modify the statistics display on that specific tab only.

storage

Save your preferences, authentication tokens, and edited values locally in your browser. This data stays on your device.

scripting

Inject scripts that modify the visual display of statistics on OnlyFans pages. This is the core functionality of the extension.

alarms

Periodically check your license status in the background to ensure uninterrupted service without impacting browser performance.

Host Permission: onlyfans.com

Required to enable the core functionality - editing statistics display on OnlyFans pages only.
